Collaboration Principles

Every collaboration rests on a few simple agreements:
  • Sovereignty. We design in a way that protects your voice, your people, and your data.
  • Honesty. If it’s not a fit — in timing, capacity, or scope — that will be named clearly.
  • Depth over volume. Fewer, better collaborations, not a constant churn of projects.
  • Care for capacity. Work moves at a sustainable pace. No burnout worship here.
