Build Guide

1
Curate

Start with 12–30 strong works. Group by collection, medium, theme, or year so the visitor never feels lost.

2
Document

Add title, medium, year, dimensions, status, price/inquire note, and a short story. This creates collector confidence.

3
Style

Choose a gallery mood. White Cube is clean, Velvet Night is dramatic, Parchment feels archival, Botanical feels warm, Editorial is sharp.

4
Export

Download your gallery HTML and JSON backup. Keep dated copies so your portfolio has provenance over time.
